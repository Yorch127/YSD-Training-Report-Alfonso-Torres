Section 2 — Topics Covered
  • 1. Aerial Lift Safety Covered
    • Pre-use inspection requirements
    • Load capacity and platform stability
    • Fall protection requirements while on lift
    • Overhead and ground hazard awareness
    • Emergency lowering and rescue procedures
  • 2. Fall Protection Covered
    • OSHA fall protection requirements (29 CFR 1926.502)
    • Types of fall protection systems (guardrails, PFAS, covers)
    • Harness inspection, fit, and donning procedure
    • Anchor points, tie-off requirements, and D-ring placement
    • Leading edge work and floor/hole coverage
  • 3. Ladder Safety Covered
    • Ladder selection and pre-use inspection
    • Proper setup: angle, footing, and securing method
    • Three-point contact rule and body positioning
    • Safely carrying tools and materials while climbing
    • Weight limits, load ratings, and ANSI duty types
